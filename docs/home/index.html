Yuantong Li (李沅桐)

I’m a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics, Purdue University. My advisor is Prof. Guang Cheng. During my master period, I was a visiting scholar at Cornell University (with Prof. Fei Wang), OSU (with Prof. Huan Sun), and UPenn (with Prof. Rui Feng).

My research interests fall in Statistical Reinforcement Learning: multi-armed bandit and online Learning.
